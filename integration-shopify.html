Setup Guide

Connect in 5 steps

No developer needed. Go from install to live content in under 15 minutes.

1

Install the Performative AI app

Find us in the Shopify App Store and click "Add app". No credit card required to start your 14-day free trial.

Takes about 30 seconds.

2

Grant catalogue access

Approve read/write permissions for products and metafields. We only request what we need — your orders and customer data stay private.

3

Choose your AI profile

Select your brand tone (professional, casual, technical) and target language. Pick which fields to generate: titles, descriptions, meta, bullet points, alt text.

4

Run a bulk generation

Select all products or a filtered subset. Click "Generate" and watch Performative AI write content for every SKU — typically 1,000 products in under 4 minutes.

Preview before publishing — nothing goes live without your approval.

5

Enable auto-sync for new products

Turn on the auto-sync rule so every new product added to Shopify gets AI content generated and published within seconds — completely hands-free.

⚡ What happens behind the scenes

  • ① We read your product title, vendor, tags, and existing description.
  • ② Our model identifies category, key features, and target audience.
  • ③ We generate all requested fields with your brand voice applied.
  • ④ Content is checked against your keyword list and SEO rules.
  • ⑤ Approved content is pushed directly to Shopify via API — live instantly.

AI Workflow

From catalogue to live content

Our four-stage pipeline ensures every product gets relevant, accurate, high-converting content — at any scale.

📥

Ingest

We pull all product data from Shopify — titles, tags, vendor, images, existing descriptions, and all metafields.

🧠

Analyse

Our AI classifies each product by category, audience, and intent. It identifies missing information and cross-references your brand guide.

✍️

Generate

All requested fields are written simultaneously — titles, descriptions, meta, bullets, alt text — with your tone and keywords applied throughout.

🚀

Publish

Reviewed content is pushed directly to Shopify via the Admin API. Changes go live in seconds, no export/import required.

Data & Security

Your data stays yours

We take a privacy-first approach. Your product data is used to generate content and nothing else.

🔒

Minimal permissions

We only request read access to products and write access to product content fields. Orders, customers, and financial data are never accessed.

🛡️

No training on your data

Your product catalogue is never used to train our models. All processing happens in isolated, ephemeral environments that are wiped after each job.

🌍

GDPR & SOC 2 ready

All data is processed in EU or US regions based on your preference. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant for enterprise customers.
