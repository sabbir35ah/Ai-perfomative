FanQ scaled to 48,000 products
with zero copywriters

How Europe's largest sports merchandise retailer automated product content across 14 languages — and grew organic search revenue by 92% in 8 months.

Company

FanQ

Industry

Sports Merchandise

Platform

Shopify Plus

Products

48,000 SKUs

Timeline

8 months

The challenge

48,000 products. 14 languages. No budget for a content team.

FanQ had grown rapidly through acquisitions, inheriting catalogues from three separate retailers. The result was 48,000 products — many with placeholder descriptions, thin content, or content copy-pasted directly from manufacturer data feeds.

Their SEO team flagged the issue: Google was penalising thin, duplicate content across thousands of product URLs, suppressing FanQ's visibility for high-value commercial search terms.

Hiring a content team to rewrite 48,000 product pages in 14 languages was not commercially viable — estimated cost: €1.2M and 3+ years to complete. They needed a different approach.

The solution

Performative AI across the entire catalogue, in every language.

  • ✓ Integrated Performative AI with Shopify Plus via our Enterprise API in 2 days.
  • ✓ Configured a custom brand voice profile reflecting FanQ's energetic, fan-first tone.
  • ✓ Ran the full 48,000 SKU bulk generation over a weekend — completed in 18 hours.
  • ✓ Auto-translated all content into 14 languages with regional tone adaptation.
  • ✓ Enabled auto-sync so new product drops get content within 60 seconds of being added.
  • ✓ Connected keyword lists to ensure all descriptions target priority search terms.
